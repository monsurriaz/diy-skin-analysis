Start Up Instructions for BP Gel

You can start your BP 10% Medicated Gel tonight. Only use Â½ a  pump. You will use the BP every other night only for 10 nights. By the 6th night you can use a whole pump. The BP will fight bumps and will also reduce your oiliness.  After 10 nights if you are not dry, irritated, or red you can begin to use it every night. 1 pump only. After two weeks if you have a lot of acne and it goes down your jaw or neck you can use 2 pumps.
***After three weeks stop using the cleanser in the mornings. Use only water and a washcloth in the morning followed by the BP every morning.  After 1 week of using the BP in the morning and in the evening if you are oily you can reintroduce the cleanser back into your mornings. At this point you would be using the cleanser AM and PM and the 10%BP in the AM and PM as well.

If you feel that the BP is highly irritating you can check for sensitivity on the inside of your forearm. Apply a bit of BP 10% Medicated Gel on your inner forearm every night for 5 nights. If you begin to get red, itchy or swollen stop the product application-you are too sensitive to use benzoyl peroxide of any formulation.
